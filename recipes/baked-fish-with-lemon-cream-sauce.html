Baked fish with lemony creamy sauce!


Effortless, tender, lemony and creamy - obviously.

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 x 150-180g Fish Fillets about 1.5cm thick, skinless and boneless
  • 4 tbsp Unsalted Butter unsalted
  • 1/4 cup Heavy Cream (can be substituted with evaporated milk for a healthier option)
  • 2 garlic cloves minced
  • 1 tbsp Dijon Mustard
  • 1.5 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1.5 tbsp eschallots finely chopped
  • Salt & Pepper
