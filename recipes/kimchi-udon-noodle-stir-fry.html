Udon noodle stir fry... now with kimchi!


Juicy, garlicy, savory, delicious.

INGREDIENTS

Main
  • 2 packs Udon Noodles total 400g
  • 4 slices Rindless Short Cut Bacon chopped into thumbnail size pieces, 120g
  • 1 cup aged Kimchi chopped into thumbnail size pieces
  • 50g Onion thinly sliced
  • 30g Green Onion thinly sliced
  • Cooking Oil
Sauce mix these in a bowl
Garnish optional
